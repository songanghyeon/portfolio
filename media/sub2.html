SYDNEY

Sydney the largest city in Australia and the capital of New South Wales, is renowned for its stunning harbor, iconic landmarks, vibrant culture, and diverse population. As one of the most visited cities in the world, Sydney offers a unique blend of natural beauty and cosmopolitan charm. history dates back over 30,000 years, with its original inhabitants being the Aboriginal people. The city was officially founded in 1788 by Captain Arthur Phillip, who led the First Fleet of British ships carrying convicts to establish a penal colony. This marked the beginning of European settlement in Australia.

Melburne

Melbourne is a sacred place for trends and restaurants in Australia. In Melbourne, you'll find a wealth of opportunities to experience the arts as well as top-notch dining and exciting sporting events. Melburne is a perfect blend of rich cultural history and trends of the new era. As the sun goes down, the city comes to life as countless restaurants as well as various events and exhibitions are crowded with people. Experience the taste of Melburne while exploring crowded alleys, trendy areas, and stylish restaurant areas.

BONDI

Bondi Beach is one of the most iconic and well-known beaches in Australia, located in the suburb of Bondi, just 7 kilometers east of Sydney's central business district. Known for its stunning sandy shoreline, vibrant atmosphere, and world-class surfing conditions, Bondi Beach attracts millions of visitors each year from all over the globe. Known for its golden sand and clear sea water that stretches for about a kilometer, it is a paradise for surfers, swimmers, and sunbathers. The beaches are patrolled by LifeGuard to ensure the safety of visitors. Besides the beaches, Bondi is also known for its vibrant beach culture. The coastal trail, which runs from Bondi to Coogee, offers stunning views of the coastline and is one of the most popular activities for visitors, including Tamarama Beach and Brontë Beach.
