Vivid Sydney

Vivid Sydney is one of Australia's most glamorous and famous festivals, first launched in 2009 and held annually as a festival of lights, music, and ideas to showcase Sydney's artistic talent and technological advancement. Combining art, technology, and commerce, it attracts millions of visitors from all over the world every year with a dazzling feast of light, music, and ideas.

New Year's Eve

Sydney New Year's Eve is one of the world's most iconic celebrations, renowned for its spectacular fireworks display. Held annually on December 31st, the event takes place around Sydney Harbour, including the Sydney Harbour Bridge and Sydney Opera House. The festivities attract over a million spectators and millions more watching on television globally.

Australian Open

The Australian Open is a world-famous tennis competition held every January in Melbourne, Australia. It is one of four Grand Slam tournaments along with Wimbledon. It is considered one of the most prestigious competitions in the world, including the French Open, the U.S. Open, and tennis. For tennis fans, the Australian Open offers the opportunity to watch top-notch games at the start of the year, marking an important and challenging start to the tennis season for players.

Hamilton Island Race Week

Hamilton Island Race Week is a world-renowned yacht racing event held annually in August on Hamilton Island, located in Queensland, Australia. Established in 1984, it has become one of the largest and most prestigious sailing events in Australia. During the race week, a wide variety of yachts compete in the pristine waters around the stunning Whitsunday Islands. Participants range from professional sailors to passionate amateurs, all showcasing their skills in this thrilling maritime competition. The diverse fleet of yachts and the competitive spirit make for an exhilarating spectacle.
